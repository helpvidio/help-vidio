Why do I need help videos?

Why not make them yourself? It's not worth your time.

I also want your business, but really it's not worth your time. It may seem quick and easy, but it isn't, something I learned the hard way when I first got assigned to create videos as an intern.

Here's some of the things you need...
  • Audio and video editing software
  • Audio setup (microphone, etc.)
  • A quiet place
  • Time
  • Practice
  • More time

The bare minimum in equipment alone will put you out a few hundred dollars. The real cost though is time. Videos are a great example of the Pareto principle. Getting the video perfect takes a huge amount of time and effort. It takes practice, you'll screw up over and over before you finally get it.
