The Process

  1. Fill out the order form and get a quote
  2. Pay 50% up front
  3. Fill out a form giving any further information
  4. I take your help docs and make them scripts staying as close to word for word as possible
  5. I upload your videos in Cage where you'll be able to offer feedback
  6. You like the videos and pay the final 50%
  7. I setup video hosting on your app of choice and upload/deliver the videos. (or just send the videos if preferred)

Pricing

$150 for your first video, $100 for every video after

The first video costs more because of the initial setup time it takes to get rolling.

These prices are assuming

  1. You have help docs already written which will be used as scripts
  2. You have a version of your application where videos can be easily recorded without interference.

If the above is not the case extra fees will be charged.

I reserve the right to turn down clients or charge extra if I believe videos will require extra work.
